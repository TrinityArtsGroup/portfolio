"Red Harvest" (1929) Video Game Screen

With my deep knowledge of film and its history, I decided to envision a game I would have loved to see
growing up in the 1990’s – an adaptation of the book that started an entire genre! Dashiell Hammett’s
classic "Red Harvest"“Red Harvest”, and its enigmatic main character – known colloquially as
‘the Continental Op’. As of 2025, Hammet’s book and the “Op” character have fallen into the public
domain.
